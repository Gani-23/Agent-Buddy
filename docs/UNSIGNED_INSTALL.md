# Installing Unsigned Builds (Windows + macOS)

These builds are **unsigned**, so Windows and macOS will show a warning the first time you open them.
This is normal. Follow the steps below to open the app safely.

---

## Windows (Unknown Publisher)

1. Double‑click the app.
2. When Windows shows **“Windows protected your PC”**, click **More info**.
3. Click **Run anyway**.

---

## macOS (Cannot verify developer)

1. Right‑click the app and choose **Open**.
2. macOS will show a warning — click **Open** again.

If macOS blocks it completely:
1. Go to **System Settings → Privacy & Security**.
2. Scroll to **Security** and click **Open Anyway**.

---

## Why this happens

Apple and Microsoft require paid certificates to remove these warnings.
We ship unsigned builds to keep the app free and simple.
